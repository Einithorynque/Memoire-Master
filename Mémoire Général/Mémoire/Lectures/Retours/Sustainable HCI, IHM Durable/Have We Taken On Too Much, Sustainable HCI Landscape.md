## Résumé :


L'article fait état des critiques de l'IHM Durable et met en avant les principales. Il montre si l'IHM Durable a évolué face à elles et propose des axes de recherche pertinents.

### Critiques et recos

> [!quote] Critique du design persuasif
> - les utilisateurs n'agissent pas de manière rationnelles 
> - il est faux de penser engendrer un comportement par un appareil sans que son contexte d'usage n'interfère, dans le cas d'un changement de comportement écologique la persuasion ne fonctionne pas et tend à s'effacer au quotidien (page 1, colonne 2)
> « More specifically, the community has understood the limitations of persuasive, individual-focused projects and moved towards more diverse, qualitative and speculative SHCI research. » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 8)

> [!quote] Sortir du solutionnisme, la tech ne peut tout régler
> Transformer notre monde en monde durable dépend d'énormément de facteur, le numérique ne peut pas se vendre comme la solution et doit comprendre le peu d'impacte qu'il a concrètement
> 
> Quelques solutions possibles :
>- «Other suggestions include “having ‘enough’ as a central design theme for applications that support communication and information access” [34], » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 5)
> - « focus on a generative, positive theme of more to counter limits » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 5)

> [!Tip] Sortir du "petit geste" individuel pour avoir un impact
> Chercher une amélioration de l'activisme 
> 
> « The main idea is that interactive technologies can be used to address broader levels of community engagement [46] and societal transformation [30] rather than to target each user only within the context of their personal life: » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 5)
> 
> « ==Supporting people to engage in activist behaviours [30] is thereby seen as a particularly promising strategy as these behaviours, together with major lifestyles changes, “are ultimately the behaviours that have the potential for signifcant impacts for sustainability.”== » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 5)
> 
> **Mais** « Such calls raise important questions, like those about the attribution of responsibility [23] and protection of democracy: “Can we identify ways to activate individuals and communities without jeopardizing that which makes democracy worth protecting, that is, without bringing to life some grotesque version of a (more or or less) benevolent eco-dictatorship?” [7]. » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 5)
> - Je dois faire en sorte que chacun puisse exprimer sa voie pour valoriser le changement au lieu de juste mettre en avant celle des design
> - Je suis maintenant autant militant que chercheur / les institutions qui me financent doivent suivre, m'enfin..
> - Si des groupes d'activistes dépendent de mon travail, comment ça marche sur le temps long
>   
> « Our review of the SHCI critiques is telling a story: ==technologists cannot be expected to do the work of policy makers.== Even if our community would fnd ways to acquire all the skills that are called for and be fully equipped to take on the underlying structural challenges to society, it is not us, but the policy makers that need to implement these changes. [...] it means that we should challenge the narrative that we can rely on technology to save us, just as we challenged the narrative that climate change is an individual behaviour change problem. » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 8)

> [!Quote] Système économique
> 
> L'IHM durable est en tension dans un système économique du numérique basé sur la croissance. Il s'inscrit donc dans un système économique centré sur des valeurs (ici écologique).
> 
> « some have called for a new model for the digital economy, with “technologies relying less on instrumental purposes of eficiency connected with corporate profit... (motivated by research paradigms grounded in the belief of infnite economic growth) and relying more on volitional and value-laden aspects underlying people’s use of technologies” [51] (quoting Naomi Klein [50]). » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 6)



### Green Policy informatics

Imaginer le rôle de SHCI dans un monde où les choix politique auraient lieux pour contrer la crise climatique > quel serait mon rôle. **Penser le numérique durable comme un moyen pour assurer le bras de fer permettant des politiques écoloos** 

> « If bold and ambitious climate policy were in place, could SHCI constructively apply a more familiar and well-honed skillset to help realise those policies in a whole host of domains? We think so. Let’s call this pathway ‘Green Policy informatics’. » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 8)

Mon rôle pourrait être : 
- Aider aux prises de décisions complexes en contrant les incertitudes. 
- Montrer la responsabilité des actions politiques

> « This could provide new focus for SHCI. We could help ensure digital systems bring needed transparent accounting and accountability, support complex decision making under uncertainty, and enable the deconstruction of popular myths about the energy and carbon impacts of everyday life. » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 8)

> [!Tip] Rendre compte du monde par le numérique
> « Rather, as Dourish refects: “interactive systems are also lenses through which we encounter the world” [23], framed by broader social, political and economic processes. As such, as Green Policy informaticians, we have a potentially critical role in addressing the urgent problems of environmental sustainability. » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 9)

> We need all of HCI’s holistic design skills to get the message of Green Policy informatics across to policy makers. We need to drive policy makers to meet their responsibility when it comes to climate change mitigation and implementation of socially, economically and environmentally just and sustainable systems. 
### Etat de l'art

> « The papers in our corpus frequently view environmental and other kinds of sustainability, notably social and economic sustainability, as interlinked. » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 7)

La nature systémique (un tout de plein d'influence) des comportements utilisateurs vis à vis du climat est acquise 
En découle de nombreuses études plus **qualitatives** que quantitatives (interview, workshop)

Avoiding such prescriptions allows us to get on with our work instead of philosophising over metrics and defnitions. » (Bremer et al., 2022, p. 7)
